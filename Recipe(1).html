M&M Cookie

The M&M cookie is a delicious treat that combines a soft and chewy cookie base with a burst of color and sweetness from the vibrant M&M chocolate pieces. With each bite, you'll experience the perfect harmony of chocolatey goodness and crunchy M&M shells.
  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 12 minutes
  • Total Time: 32 minutes
  • Servings: 10-20 depending on size
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups M&M's (any variety)
    • Instructions:
  • 1. Preheat your oven to 175Â°C. Line two baking sheets with baking paper
  • 2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy using a hand mixer or stand mixer.
  • 3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  • 4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  • 5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined. Do not overmix the dough.
  • 6. Gently fold in the M&M's, reserving a few to press on top of the cookies later.
  • 7. Using a cookie scoop or a spoon, drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between each cookie.
  • 8. Press a few additional M&M's on top of each cookie for a more attractive appearance.
  • 9. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  • 10. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
  • 11. Enjoy your scrumptious M&M cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite beverage!

    • Note: Feel free to customize the recipe by using different varieties of M&M's , such as peanut butter, peanut, or pretzel M&M's. You can also add chopped nuts or chocolate chips to the cookie dough for extra texture and flavor.
