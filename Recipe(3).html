Lofthouse Cookie

Lofthouse Cookies are renowned for their soft, cake-like texture and vibrant, eye-catching frosting topped with sprinkles. These popular cookies are often enjoyed on special occasions and holidays, delighting taste buds with their unique blend of sweetness and indulgence.
  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 7 minutes
  • Total Time: 27 minutes
  • Servings: 24
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Powdered sugar for dusting (optional)
    • Instructions:
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with baking paper.
  • 2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  • 3. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  • 4. Mix in the lemon zest, fresh lemon juice, and vanilla extract until well combined. The mixture might look curdled at this stage, but that's normal.
  • 5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • 6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  • 7. Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between each cookie.
  • 8. Gently flatten each cookie slightly with the back of the spoon or your fingertips.
  • 9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes, or until the edges of the cookies are just beginning to turn golden. The centers should remain soft.
  • 10. Remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  • 11. If desired, dust the cooled cookies with powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness and decoration.
  • 12. Enjoy your homemade lemon cookies with a cup of tea or as a delightful treat anytime!
    •

    These lemon cookies are sure to bring a burst of citrusy goodness to your taste buds. Feel free to adjust the level of lemon zest and juice according to your preference for a stronger or milder lemon flavor.
