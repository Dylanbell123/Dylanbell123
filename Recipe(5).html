Double Choc Chip Cookie

Double chocolate chip cookies are indulgent treats that boast a rich cocoa-infused dough studded with an abundant amount of chocolate chips, offering a double dose of chocolatey delight in every bite. These cookies satisfy both cocoa enthusiasts and cookie lovers with their irresistible blend of deep chocolate flavor and gooey, melty chocolate goodness.
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 8 minutes
  • Total Time: 18 minutes
  • Servings: 24
